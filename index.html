About Me


Hi, I'm Jen and I love programming!


I graduated from Virginia Tech with a degree in fashion merchandising and design, but really craved more critical-thinking in my day to day life. I was introduced to programming through my friends and have fell in love with it ever since. I love being able to use both my creative and problem solving sides to create new projects.


I am always trying to learn new things. Currently, I am learning Python and hope to have a project to show off on my portfolio soon!


When I'm not programming, you can find me playing animal crossing, knitting sweaters, and eating a lot of food.
